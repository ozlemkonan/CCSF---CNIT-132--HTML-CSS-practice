Intro to Front End Engineering w/JavaScript

Hosted by Ryan Lee and Mike Sweeney (PunchCode)

What are the objectives of this meetup?
  1. JavaScript overview: The first thirty minutes will be an introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
    • How is it different from other languages?
    • What type of applications and problems is it typically used for?
    • What resources are available online to learn at home?
    • What is PunchCode's Full-Stack Engineering program like?
  2. JavaScript hands-on: The second portion will be about an hour to an hour and a half of walk through to create a simple game and get you started in HTML, CSS, and end in JavaScript. A free crash course in Front End Engineering - because knowledge is power!
What did I get?
  • JS in dynamically typed that makes it faster to develop small scripts.
  • Creating enterprise apps without type-checking can get messy though.
  • JS programs can create that run independently of the browser

→ PunchCode is a non-profit coding school that offers many full-time programs for software development, it also provide free introductory courses to become a full-time programmer just yet.


Lessons learned from transitioning to a remote engineering team based in LatAm

Hosted by James Severance (Hire Front End Developers)

What are the objectives of this meetup?

PresencePG and Tango as they talk about their experience working with high-performant devs in Mexico.

This event is designed for decision-makers, from engineering managers to CTOs to early-stage founders, who are exploring outsourcing / nearshore options and would be interested in learning more about the Mexican developer market.

What did I get?
  • What to expect when hiring and managing people around the world? → First of all, recruiters want employer to communicate with enough English.
  • How to fuel a positive culture for remote workers? → Proper strategy, open lines of communication and empathy. Respect to time diffrenece and physical conditions that employers have.
  • Strategies for attracting, engaging, and retaining top talent → Give them opportunity to work or to join conferences in Silicon Valley.

→ Also I got all of the speakers included Jason Monberg (Ceo of Presence), Eric Slegried (Ceo of Tango), Eric Muller (CTO of Presence) contact informations.
